Property Description

Nestled in one of Ascot’s most prestigious neighborhoods, the swimming pool side of this stunning residential home is a true highlight of luxury living. Surrounded by manicured gardens and elegant landscaping, the pool area offers a peaceful retreat from the everyday. Whether it's for morning laps or a relaxing evening dip, the design seamlessly blends comfort with sophistication, making it the perfect place to unwind or entertain guests.

The pool itself is crafted with premium materials, featuring crystal-clear water, modern tiling, and soft ambient lighting that brings the space to life after sunset. Comfortable loungers, shaded pergolas, and a poolside dining area create a resort-like atmosphere, allowing homeowners to enjoy both leisure and lifestyle without ever leaving home. The connection between indoor and outdoor living is enhanced through large sliding glass doors that open directly to the pool area from the main living spaces.

This Ascot home exemplifies how thoughtful design can elevate outdoor living. The poolside is not only a functional extension of the home but also a visual masterpiece that adds to its overall charm. Perfect for family gatherings, weekend barbecues, or tranquil solo moments, it captures the essence of contemporary elegance in a natural setting. The swimming pool side of this residence is more than an amenity — it’s a lifestyle statement.
